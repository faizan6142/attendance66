About AttenZen

AttenZen is a multi-course attendance system built to help academic teams record, review, and report attendance with less manual work and clearer insights.

Centralized Management

Manage courses, semesters, subjects, and students from one interface.

Clear Reporting

Track attendance trends with percentage summaries and downloadable reports.

Role-Based Access

Support admin and student experiences with separate dashboards and controls.

Why this site exists

This website was designed to reduce spreadsheet overhead and make attendance workflows reliable for both instructors and students. It combines quick daily marking with easy term-level analysis.
